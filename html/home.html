Getting Started

To get started, you need a Personal Online Datastore (POD). Please choose one from the select box below the logo.

Once you have signed up for a POD, you can login and click the VIEW PROFILE button.

You can edit your profile from the font-end, although you will need to use the Pixolid photo manager to edit your photos. But don't worry, you don't need to create a new account!

About DVO

DVO is short for "Devolution". It is a suite of applications that are designed to give people more control over their lives, whether it is more control over their data, the content on the internet, or the communities in which they live.
