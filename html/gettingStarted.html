Getting Started

To get started, you need a Personal Online Datastore (POD). Clicking on this link will pick a POD provider at random. However, if you prefer, you can choose one from the list (under the logo).

Once you have signed up for a POD, you will be able to see your page in action.

You can edit your profile from the font-end, although you will need to use the Pixolid photo manager to edit your photos. But don't worry, you don't need to create a new account!
